Don Quixote
was published in 1605 and 1615 by
Miguel de Cervantes Saavedra.
It was translated from Spanish in 1885 by
John Ormsby.

This ebook was produced for
Standard Ebooks
by
Alex Cabal,
and is based on a transcription produced in 2004 by
David Widger
for
Project Gutenberg,
on transcriptions of footnotes produced in 2015 by
Alex Cabal,
and on digital scans from the
HathiTrust Digital Library.

The cover page is adapted from
Don Quixote and the Dead Mule,
a painting completed circa 1864 by
Honoré Daumier.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
